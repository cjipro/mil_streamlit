Intelligence principles

Evidence, not impression.

01
Verbatim customer voice — always.
Customer quotes are never paraphrased, trimmed for tone, or reframed. What the reader sees is what the customer wrote, labelled with source and date. No synthesised voices, ever.
02
Signal separation, by design.
Reckoner and Sonar use public market data — no customer PII collected, no private data exchanged. Pulse runs inside your environment on your telemetry only. Signals never cross.
03
Anchored to sector precedent.
Every Sonar and Reckoner finding is anchored to the CJI Chronicle — a public ledger of real sector failures researched in depth. Patterns that rhyme with precedent surface faster, with receipts.
