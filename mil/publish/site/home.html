Research principles

Evidence, not impression.

01
Public signal only, or your signal only.
Sonar uses public market data — no customer PII collected, no private data exchanged. Pulse runs inside your environment on your telemetry only. The two never cross.
02
Verbatim customer voice.
Customer quotes are never paraphrased, trimmed for tone, or reframed. What the reader sees is what the customer wrote, labelled with source and date.
03
Anchored to sector precedent.
Every finding is anchored to a chronicled sector failure — a ledger of real incidents we've researched in depth. Patterns that rhyme with precedent surface faster.
